Case Study 03

Lead-to-Admission Funnel Management Using LeadSquared CRM

Overview

Managed and supported a structured lead-to-admission flow using LeadSquared CRM, covering qualification, counselling, program fitment, objection handling, payment support, admission closure, and post-admission follow-up.

Business Context

Higher education admissions involve multiple touchpoints across calls, walk-ins, and virtual counselling. Each lead requires timely communication and clear stage tracking.

Objective

Ensure leads move through a clear sales funnel with proper qualification, counselling, follow-ups, and admission-support actions tracked in CRM.

Funnel Flow

Lead Received → Qualification → Counselling → Program Fitment → Objection Handling → Payment / Loan Support → Admission Closure → Post-Admission Follow-up

My Role

Used LeadSquared CRM to support lead tracking, follow-up planning, pipeline updates, counselling continuity, and admission process coordination.

Key Challenges

  • Managing lead follow-ups across different counselling channels.
  • Identifying correct program fitment based on prospect goals.
  • Handling concerns related to timing, affordability, and decision confidence.
  • Keeping CRM pipeline updates consistent and actionable.

Actions Taken

  • Tracked lead movement from received stage to qualification and counselling.
  • Supported program fitment discussions through calls, walk-ins, and virtual counselling.
  • Handled objections through structured communication and follow-up discipline.
  • Assisted with payment or education loan process support before admission closure.
  • Maintained post-admission follow-up for continuity and support.

Outcome

Helped maintain a more structured admission funnel with clearer lead visibility, timely follow-ups, stronger counselling continuity, and better process ownership.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Lead qualification
  • LeadSquared CRM usage
  • Pipeline management
  • Sales follow-up tracking
  • Admissions closure support

Key Learning

CRM discipline is most valuable when it supports real counselling action, follow-up clarity, and a better experience for the prospect throughout the admission journey.
